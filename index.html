Join us on our mission to reshape the future of business through intelligent automation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Twenty One AI and how it can benefit your business.

How long does it take to implement Twenty One AI?

Implementation timelines vary based on your specific needs and complexity. Most businesses can get started with basic automation workflows within 1-2 weeks. Our expert team provides comprehensive onboarding and training to ensure a smooth transition. For more complex custom implementations, we typically work with you over 4-12 weeks to build and optimize your AI solutions. We pride ourselves on quick time-to-value, with many clients seeing tangible results within the first month of deployment.

Is my data secure with Twenty One AI?

Absolutely. Security is our top priority. We employ enterprise-grade encryption (AES-256) for all data in transit and at rest. Our infrastructure is hosted on AWS with compliance certifications including SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, and HIPAA. We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing to ensure the highest standards of data protection. Your data is never shared with third parties without explicit consent, and you maintain complete ownership and control over all your information. We also offer on-premise deployment options for organizations with specific security requirements.

Can Twenty One AI integrate with our existing systems?

Yes, absolutely. Twenty One AI is designed to seamlessly integrate with your existing technology stack. We offer pre-built integrations with popular platforms like Salesforce, HubSpot, SAP, Oracle, and hundreds of other business applications. For systems without pre-built connectors, our flexible API and webhook capabilities allow for custom integrations. Our integration specialists work closely with your team to ensure smooth data flow and minimal disruption to your operations. Most integrations can be set up in hours or days, not weeks.

What kind of support does Twenty One AI provide?

We provide comprehensive support across all tiers. All customers receive access to our knowledge base, community forums, and email support. Premium tiers include 24/7 phone and chat support, dedicated account managers, and priority response times (1-4 hours depending on tier). We also offer comprehensive training programs, including webinars, documentation, and hands-on workshops. Our support team is highly trained and committed to helping you maximize the value of Twenty One AI. Many customers also benefit from our professional services team for custom development and optimization.

What is the pricing model for Twenty One AI?

We offer flexible pricing models to suit businesses of all sizes. Our Starter plan begins at $499/month and includes basic automation features for small teams. The Professional plan ($1,999/month) adds advanced analytics and priority support. Our Enterprise plan offers custom pricing based on your specific needs and usage. We also offer a 14-day free trial so you can experience the full platform before committing. Most customers see their investment pay for itself within 2-3 months through cost savings and efficiency gains. We're happy to work with you to find a pricing tier that fits your budget and delivers maximum ROI.
